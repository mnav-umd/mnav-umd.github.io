Welcome!

I am an Assistant Policy Adviser and Economist at the Federal Reserve Bank of Atlanta. Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at the National Bureau of Economic Research. Before joining the NBER, I was the inaugural Alice Rivlin Dissertation Fellow at the Hutchins Center on Fiscal and Monetary Policy. I was also an economist at the U.S. Census Bureau, where I worked on projects designed to modernize key economic statistics such as inflation using scanner-level data. I continue to work on the RESET project, which is supported by grants from the Alfred P. Sloan Foundation.

I received my Ph.D. in Economics from the University of Maryland in 2024. I received my BA in Economics and French from Williams College in 2016. To contact me directly, please email me at mnavarrete.econ@gmail.com.
